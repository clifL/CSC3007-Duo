The Singapore Story

Its been more than 2 years since the first locally transmitted case was reported in Singapore. Let's take a look at how the situation has evolved.

Singapore has been experiencing a rise in Covid-19 cases with a majority of the case belonging to the 20 - 39 age group.

Did you know?

Case numbers on Tuesdays tend to be higher compared with the rest of the week as they reflect the spike in infections after the weekend, when more people are out in various social settings.
